Home Terms

Meet Messenger (“we,” “us,” or “our”) is operated by Brogrammers Agency. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information when you use our website and services.

Information We Collect

  • Account Information: name, email address, and login credentials.
  • Usage Information: messages, meeting activity, feature usage, and diagnostics.
  • Device Information: device type, browser, IP address, and approximate location.
  • SMS Information: phone number, message content, and delivery metadata.

How We Use Information

  • Provide and improve Meet Messenger features and support.
  • Send service notifications, reminders, and account alerts.
  • Maintain security, prevent abuse, and enforce our terms.

SMS & Mobile Information

If you opt in to SMS, we will send you service-related messages. Message frequency varies. Message and data rates may apply. You can opt out at any time by replying STOP.

No mobile information will be shared with third parties/affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes. Information sharing to subcontractors in support of the service, such as customer service or technical support providers, is permitted. All other categories exclude text messaging originator opt-in data and consent; this information will not be shared with any third parties.

How We Share Information

  • Service Providers: trusted vendors who help us operate the service.
  • Legal Requirements: if required by law or to protect rights and safety.
  • Business Transfers: in connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets.

Data Retention

We retain information for as long as needed to provide the service and comply with legal obligations. You can request deletion by contacting us.

Your Choices

  • Opt out of SMS by replying STOP.
  • Update account details within your profile settings.
